I am a second-year master’s student at USC, currently conducting research at HaRVI Lab. I received my bachelor’s degree from the School of Computer and Information Technology at Beijing Jiaotong University.

I’ve previously spent wonderful summers at BJTU INSIS and MIT CSGF, and was fortunate to intern at CASIA and Lenovo. Through these experiences, I developed a strong interest in computer vision and reinforcement learning.

I have also volunteered as a teacher both in China and the United States, and as a volunteer at the Beijing Zoo—experiences that I deeply cherish and find truly joyful.
